FABLINKS

Fablinks was originally established in 2005, evolved from “Silkentop,” and created by combining “Fabric” and “Links.” While SINTEX (Sinyang Globiz since 1994) mainly focused on bridal, bridesmaid, mother of bride, and some prom & evening dresses, Fablinks expanded into fancy items such as pleats, burnout, glitters, foil, velvet, and party/afternoon/evening dresses that are less common in Korea.
